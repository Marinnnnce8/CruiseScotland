At Cruise Scotland we are working closely with all our members as we do everything in our power to protect against Covid-19. It’s our mission to ensure your passengers are in safe hands during this challenging and uncertain time.

A safe arrival at all our ports

We are committed to ensuring a safe arrival at all our ports the length and breadth of the country. From our capital Edinburgh to our most northerly port in Lerwick, Shetland, we will make sure everyone employs the same safety standards to protect against Covid-19 so that your passengers are free to enjoy all the charm and beauty Scotland has to offer.

A safe arrival at all our ports

We are proactively ensuring the highest standards of safety possible for the industry by liaising with the Scottish and UK Governments and key stakeholders. Working together with these parties is crucial in enabling us to help develop and deliver enhanced policy and procedures to protect against Covid-19.

Rebuilding
with confidance

As a big part of Scotland’s economy, it is of national importance that we rebuild the cruise industry safely and confidently together. We don’t shirk from this responsibility and we are doing our upmost to ensure we meet the challenges of Covid-19 head-on and adhere to the highest possible safety standards.

Consistency across all ports

At Cruise Scotland we are committed to working with all our members to deliver the upmost standards of safety and the most effective safety procedures on a consistent basis. We endeavour to make sure this is the case across the board and that all measures are in full compliance with the Government’s guidance for shipping and sea ports.

Ship to shore pledge

We are working with our cruise operator partners to ensure safety is paramount off-shore as well as on-board. Cruise Scotland members will be working with its visiting cruise operators to implement on our on-shore pledge which ensures your safety standards are matched by our safety standards.

Enjoy Scotland in safe hands

Scotland has a unique blend of scenery, history and culture and a special air of Celtic romance that’s all its own. Whether your passengers are savouring the beautiful landscapes, conquering the hills or sampling a dram by an open fire in a rustic pub, they’ll have no shortage of options.
